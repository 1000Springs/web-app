Physiochemical Diversity

Presented here is a scatter plot showing temperature versus pH for all hotsprings sampled. Each dot is colour-coded according to the relative chemical concentration or physical attribute selected (red - high concentration or elevated through to yellow - low concentration or reduced). The pH and temperature data for each dot can be ascertained by holding the cursor on the dot. Clicking the dot will link through to spring feature details.

In the near future, we hope to be able to include a similar search function for the high-level taxonomic rankings such as domain, phylum and class.
